Batman

The Shadow of Justice.

Bruce Wayne lost his innocence the night his parents were killed. That moment birthed the Batman — a relentless, calculated protector of Gotham City. He doesn’t need superpowers; fear is his weapon, discipline is his armor. With unmatched detective skills and an unbreakable moral code, Batman stands as a symbol that even the darkest night can lead to a brighter dawn.
