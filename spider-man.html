Spider-Man

With Great Power Comes Great Responsibility.

Peter Parker is the everyday teenager blessed — and burdened — with extraordinary powers. Torn between school, family, and saving lives, he’s as human as he is heroic. Spider-Man swings through the city not for fame or fortune, but because it’s the right thing to do. In every punch he throws and every joke he cracks, there’s a heart that won’t stop fighting for the underdog.
