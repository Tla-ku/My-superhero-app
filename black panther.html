Black Panther

The King Who Fights for His People.

T’Challa is not only Wakanda’s ruler — he’s its protector. As the Black Panther, he blends tradition with futuristic technology to defend his people and honor his ancestors. He is wise beyond his years, fierce in combat, and calm in leadership. When he moves, he represents more than a country — he represents a legacy of power with peace.
