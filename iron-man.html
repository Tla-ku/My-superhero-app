Iron Man

Genius. Billionaire. Hero.

Tony Stark is more than a man in a metal suit — he's a force of innovation. After surviving a life-threatening attack, he reinvented himself, creating the Iron Man armor and dedicating his mind and money to protecting the world. Behind the sarcasm and swagger is a man driven by guilt, brilliance, and an unwavering sense of responsibility. Iron Man doesn’t just build tech — he builds legacies.
