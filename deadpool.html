Deadpool

The Chaos You Didn’t See Coming.

Wade Wilson is a walking contradiction — lethal yet hilarious, unpredictable yet weirdly noble. After gaining regenerative powers, he became Deadpool, a sword-swinging, fourth-wall-breaking antihero with a twisted sense of justice. He might offend you, surprise you, or save you — sometimes all at once. Just don’t expect him to follow the rules.
